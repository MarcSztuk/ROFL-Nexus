Café Nexus - CBS's Social Media platform

Café Introduction

Café Nexus was made as an exam-project, back in 2017 by Marc Sztuk. The hopes are to have a shared platform, where students can post their events and thoughts on Copenhagen Business School.

If you're a student at CBS, feel free to come join the fun! You can join future events, post comments related to Copenhagen Business School - OR, you can even host your own event!
